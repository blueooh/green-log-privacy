Green Log Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 27, 2026

1. Information We Collect

Green Log does not collect golf scores, round information, or health data on any external server. All such data is stored only on the user's devices (iPhone, Apple Watch).

However, to display in-app advertisements and measure their effectiveness, the following information may be transmitted to our advertising SDK (Google AdMob) and Apple SKAdNetwork:

  • Advertising Identifier (IDFA): Transmitted only when the user has explicitly opted in via Apple's App Tracking Transparency (ATT). If the user denies tracking, IDFA is not transmitted.
  • Coarse device/OS information, ad impression and click events: Used for ad delivery, fraud prevention, and effectiveness measurement.

Users who have purchased "Remove Ads" (in-app purchase) will not see ads, and the ad-related data described above will not be transmitted.

2. Health Data (HealthKit)

Green Log uses Apple HealthKit to collect the following data during a round:

  • Heart Rate: Measured in real time during a round via the Apple Watch sensor.
  • Step Count: Counted from the start to the end of a round.

This data is stored only on the user's devices and is never transmitted to external servers or third parties. Health data is attached to the round record so users can review their own activity.

HealthKit access requires the user's explicit permission, which can be revoked at any time via iPhone Settings → Health → Data Access & Devices.

3. Data Storage

  • All round records and health data are stored locally on the device (UserDefaults).
  • Data syncing between iPhone and Apple Watch is performed directly between devices via Apple's WatchConnectivity framework.
  • No data is transmitted to external servers, the cloud, or third parties.

4. Third-Party Disclosure

Green Log does not provide, share, or sell users' golf scores, round information, or health data to any third party.

However, the ad-related information described in section 1 may be transmitted to Google AdMob (Google LLC) and Apple SKAdNetwork for ad delivery and effectiveness measurement, subject to each provider's privacy policy:

  • Google AdMob: https://policies.google.com/privacy
  • Apple SKAdNetwork: https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/

5. Analytics and Advertising

Green Log does not use any separate analytics tools.

For ad delivery, we use Google AdMob; for ad-effectiveness measurement, we use Apple SKAdNetwork.

In accordance with Apple's App Tracking Transparency (ATT) policy, tracking via the Advertising Identifier (IDFA) only occurs when the user has explicitly granted permission. Users can change this permission at any time via iPhone Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking.

Users who have purchased "Remove Ads" (in-app purchase) will not see ads in the app.

6. Data Deletion

Users can delete individual round records inside the app, and uninstalling the app removes all data completely. Workout data stored in HealthKit can be deleted separately via the iPhone Health app.

7. Children's Privacy

Green Log does not collect any personal information from children and is safe for users of all ages.

8. Changes to This Policy

If this policy is updated, we will announce the changes on this page.
